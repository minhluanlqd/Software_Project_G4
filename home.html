Welcome to RU Parking Garages!

Our parking garage is state of the art in automation and convience. When you arrive to our garage you will
be greeted by a valet that will acquire your information through a license scanner and license plate reader.
After your information is taken, the valet will then take your car and will give you a ticket. When you
return to the garage, the valet will verify you ticket, and retrieve you car promptly due to a maximized
parking method implemented by our garge. Thank you for choosing RU Parking, where your car is our care.
